Renew

Check you’re still eligible before you renew.

You can renew online. You’ll need to provide:

  • Your Unique Reference Number (URN). You should have this on any corresondance we have sent you about your grant
  • Your renewal date. This will be on the letter confirming your grant or on a renewal reminder

You’ll also need to explain:

  • If some of your personal details have changed
  • If your conditions or disabilities have changed
  • How any changes to your conditions or disabilities are impacting your ability to work

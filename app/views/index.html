Get help at work if you’re disabled or have a health condition (Access to Work)

Renew your grant

You can renew your Access to Work grant up to 12 weeks before the date it ends.

Check you’re still eligible before you renew.

You can renew online or by phone.

You will only need to provide your name, address and date of birth.

It would help if you could provide your unique reference number. You’ll find this on any correspondence we've sent you about your grant.

What happens when you renew

An Access to Work adviser will contact you to discuss your renewal application. They may request further information about your condition, depending on your circumstances. They will also contact your employer.

If you’re offered a new grant, you’ll be told how much you’ll get and for how long.

Renew by phone

You can renew by calling the Access to Work helpline. Make sure you have all the necessary details with you when you call.

Alternative formats

Call the Access to Work number to ask for alternative formats, such as braille, large print or audio CD.

Complaints

You can contact Access to Work to complain if you are not happy with how your case has been handled.

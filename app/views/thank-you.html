What happens next

We’ve sent your renewal application to the Access to Work team.

They'll contact you or your representitive to discuss your renewal.

When they contact you, you’ll need to tell them if any of the following have changed:

  • your conditions or disabilities
  • how your conditions or disabilities affect you at work
  • how your conditions or disabilities affect your journey to work
  • your personal details
  • your job, role or working hours
